Music Diary

Everyone loves music. I listen to a lot of different genres depending on my mood. When I’m driving my car on a cloudy or rainy day, I’ll play some mellow bedroom pop. In the gym for a heavy lift, though, I’ll do rap music. Or, on a warm and sunny day, I will always sit outside with an upbeat pop playlist.

I have some favorite albums of all time, though. In no particular order, these albums show some of my favorite artists over the years. I have given each of them a rating out of 10, with a score of 10 being a total no-skip album. I also tell you the best song from each one, in my personal opinion.
