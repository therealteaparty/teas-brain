Tea’s Avocado Toast

I have eaten this breakfast almost every day for two years straight. I think I could still eat it every day for the rest of my life.

Amount Ingredient
2 Sourdough Toast
2 Eggs
2 Grape Tomatoes
Half Avocado
Spoonful Cottage Cheese
Handful Spinach
Handful Pickled Red Onions
A dash Garlic powder, tajin, paprika, lemon pepper
  1. Toast the everything bagel seasoned sourdough toast from Aldi until it is a little crunchy.
  2. Fry the eggs over hard on a stove. Mix in the grape tomatoes, spinach, garlic powder, and lemon pepper while that’s cooking.
  3. Once the toast is done, spread the cottage cheese and sliced avocado evenly on each slice. Sprinkle the tajin and paprika on that. If you’re feeling crazy, add a little bit of hot honey too.
  4. Spread the egg on top and add the feta cheese on top of everything with some salt.

Tea’s Honey Brown Sugar Shaken Espresso

This is what I’ll make if I’m sick of my morning black coffee from a pod

Amount Ingredient
2 Shots of Espresso, any kind
1 Tbs of honey
1 Tbs brown sugar
Dash Cinnamon
12oz Milk of choice
12oz Mason jar with lid
  1. Okay, first pour your honey on the bottom of your shot glasses before pulling the espresso, so that the coffee melts into the honey.
  2. Sprinkle the cinnamon on the inside of a jar with a lid
  3. Pull the honeyed shots, then stir them in the jar with the brown sugar
  4. Add ice and close the lid, then SHAKE!
  5. Fill up the remainder of the glass with milk
